Kraken RPG

Looking to play some Tabletop Roleplaying games near Newcastle Upon Tyne?

Kraken RPG is a friendly rpg club open to everyone. We meet regularly at The Bridge Hotel pub on Tuesdays and Thursdays depending on which game your currently playing. If there's space you can even come along to multiple days.

  • Regular games a week
  • Variety of RPGs
    • Yes we run D&D games
  • 5 minutes from Newcastle Central Station
  • Limited free car parking outside
    • after 6pm
  • Nice selection of Real Ales
